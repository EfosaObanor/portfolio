Original Framework

The Integrated Impact Framework (IIF)

Developed and authored by Obanor Efosa, the IIF is a diagnostic and developmental architecture for twenty-first century leadership. It is not a prescriptive formula, but an architecture of inquiry — enabling individuals and organisations to diagnose leadership quality and identify growth pathways.

Core Distinction

"Managers are those who do things right; leaders are those who do the right things."

The IIF addresses a structural crisis: executive failure rates of 50–75% globally, rooted not in skills deficits, but in the conceptual conflation of management authority with authentic leadership capacity.
